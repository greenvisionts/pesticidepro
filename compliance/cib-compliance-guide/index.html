Registration Requirements

Every pesticide must be registered with CIB-RC. Requirements: toxicology data (acute oral/dermal/inhalation, eye/skin irritation, sub-acute), efficacy trial reports from ICAR/SAU stations in 3+ agroclimatic zones, residue data, MRL studies, technical grade specification, formulation specification, application method and uses. Registration is product-specific and non-transferable.

Manufacturing Licence

Requires Form 9 licence from state pesticide licensing authority. Requirements: premises approval (separate formulation and QC areas), qualified person (chemistry/agriculture degree + experience), equipment list, product-specific CIB approval, pollution control NOC, factory inspection by state drug controller.

Label Requirements

CIB labels must include: product name and registration number, active ingredient name and concentration, formulation type, hazard symbols (red triangle for toxic class), directions for use, first aid instructions, antidote information, storage/disposal instructions, manufacturer details, batch number, manufacturing date, expiry date, net contents, registration number.

Annual Compliance Filings

Licensed manufacturers must file: Form 5 (Annual Return of Pesticide Sales) by 31 January to CIB-RC. Monthly production register to state licensing authority. Batch-wise manufacturing records maintained for 3 years. QC records for each batch.

Inspection Preparedness

CIB and state drug controller inspections check: manufacturing licence validity, batch records completeness, QC test records, label stock vs approvals, storage conditions, waste disposal records, and Form 5 compliance. Pre-inspection checklist essential for passing inspections.
