Questions & Answers

What is the Form 5 annual return requirement?
Every licensed manufacturer must file Form 5 (Annual Return of Pesticide Sales) with CIB-RC by 31 January. PesticidePro generates Form 5 automatically from sales records.
How do I manage CIB label amendments?
Label amendments require fresh CIB approval for any change. PesticidePro tracks pending amendments, stores label history, prevents use of superseded labels.
Does it cover state-level licensing?
Yes. Tracks state licences (Form 9 manufacturer, Form 10 dealer) including Maharashtra, UP, Gujarat, Karnataka requirements.
