Questions & Answers

Is the BMR format compliant with CIB inspection requirements?
Yes. The BMR format follows CIB guidelines for batch records under Insecticides Rules 1971. Includes all required fields: batch number, formula code, raw material lots, quantities, in-process results, operator signatures, and QA release. Passed CIB inspections for customers in Maharashtra, Gujarat, and UP.
Can it handle multiple production lines?
Yes. Multiple production lines tracked simultaneously with line-specific BMR, equipment assignment, and production scheduling. Output consolidated into unified production dashboard for plant manager.
How does it handle OOS (Out of Specification) results?
When a QC result falls outside specification, the system automatically: flags the batch as quarantined, notifies QA, opens an OOS investigation record, prevents batch dispatch until QA release, and logs the incident in the batch record with full audit trail.
