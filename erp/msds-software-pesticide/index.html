Questions & Answers

Is GHS MSDS mandatory in India?
India mandated GHS-based MSDS under Manufacture, Storage and Import of Hazardous Chemical Rules 1989 (amended). Workplaces must maintain MSDS for all hazardous chemicals. Export markets have their own requirements.
How often should MSDS be updated?
Update when: new hazard information identified, regulatory classification changes, formulation changes, or 3 years since last review. GHS requires significant new hazard info communicated to recipients within 6 months.
Can I store MSDS for multiple products?
Yes — unlimited products with version history. Bulk generation available. Export as PDF, Word, or send via email/WhatsApp.
